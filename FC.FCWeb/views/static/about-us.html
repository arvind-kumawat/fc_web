FundsCorner

FundsCorner is a digital lending company which aims to provide working capital finance to small businesses in India in a simple, easy and transparent manner. Our endeavour is to partner with the SMEs to offer flexible, short term loans to meet their funding requirements so that they can focus on the business.

FundsCorner is the trade name of Rajkamal Leasing and Investments Private Limited, an NBFC Company registered with RBI.

Our Endeavor

To make Working Capital accessible to Small Businesses in a seamless manner
